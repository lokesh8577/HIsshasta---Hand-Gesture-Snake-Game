What data do we collect?

Snake Game collects the following data:

Data Type Description
Email Your email address for account creation and communication
Username Your chosen display name
Discord ID If you choose to connect your Discord account
Game Statistics Information about each game session
Settings Your currently active game settings
Activity Data How many games you've started and completed

Snake Game does NOT collect:

  • Custom game configurations (they are stored in your browser's local storage)
  • Payment information (if applicable)

If you believe a certain data type is missing from the lists above, feel free to contact us and we will answer any questions and update the privacy policy.

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Create an account
  • Play a game
  • Change settings on the website
  • Connect third-party services (like Discord)
  • Contact our support team

How will we use your data?

Snake Game collects your data so that we can:

  • Provide and personalize our services
  • Track your game progress and statistics
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Display leaderboards and rankings
  • Improve our game and services
  • Communicate with you about updates or issues

If you are found to be cheating or exploiting the website, we may store hashed versions of your username, email and/or discord id to prevent you from creating new accounts.

How do we store your data?

Snake Game securely stores your data using MongoDB with appropriate security measures including:

  • Data encryption at rest and in transit
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as described in this Privacy Policy.

What are your data protection rights?

Snake Game would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

Right Description
The right to access You have the right to request copies of your personal data
The right to rectification You have the right to request correction of inaccurate information
The right to erasure You have the right to request erasure of your personal data under certain conditions
The right to restrict processing You have the right to request restriction of processing your personal data
The right to object to processing You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data
The right to data portability You have the right to request transfer of your data to another organization

Hashed data mentioned in the "How will we use your data?" section will not be deleted, as it is essential in preventing the exploitation of the website.

Analytics

Like most websites, we use Google Analytics, which collects information that your browser sends whenever you visit the website. This data may include:

  • Internet protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Date and time of visit

THIS DATA DOES NOT CONTAIN ANY PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION. We use this information for analyzing trends, administering the site, and improving user experience.

For more information on Google Analytics' privacy policy, please visit: Google Analytics Privacy Policy

Error Tracking

We use error tracking services to help us identify and fix bugs in our game. These services may collect:

  • Information about your device and browser
  • Details about errors that occur
  • Anonymous session information to help reproduce issues

This information is used solely for the purpose of improving our services and fixing technical issues.

Advertisements

Depending on the consent you provide in this form, various pieces of information may be used to provide advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you. You can withdraw your consent at any time by updating your cookie preferences.

What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, see HTTP cookie on Wikipedia.

How do we use cookies?

Snake Game uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:

  • Keeping you signed in
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Providing relevant advertisements (if enabled)

What types of cookies do we use?

There are several different types of cookies; however, our website uses:

  • Functionality cookies - So we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences
  • Analytics cookies - To understand how users interact with our website
  • Advertising cookies - Only if you consent to personalized ads

How to manage your cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

You can manage your cookie preferences through:

  • Your browser settings
  • Our website's cookie consent manager
  • Third-party opt-out tools for advertising cookies

Privacy policies of other websites

Snake Game contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.
